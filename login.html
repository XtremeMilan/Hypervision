UPES
HYPERVISION

UPES Hypervision student chapter was established in 2011,
under the patronage of UPES. The chapter focuses on peer
mentoring and introduced an abstract of family concept
according to which a specific family number is allotted to
each student and a mentor was respectively assigned to
ensure a productive and guided overall growth of the
student in academics and personality.
